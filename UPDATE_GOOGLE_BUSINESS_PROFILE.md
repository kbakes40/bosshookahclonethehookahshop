# Update Your Google Business Profile

Your Google Business Profile is currently showing Instagram as the website. Let's update it to show your new website: **www.bosshookah.site**

---

## Steps to Update Website URL

1. **Go to Google Business Profile Manager**
   - Visit: https://business.google.com
   - Sign in with the Google account that manages The Hookah Shop

2. **Select Your Business**
   - Click on "The Hookah Shop" profile

3. **Edit Business Information**
   - Click "Edit profile" or the pencil icon
   - Look for the "Website" field

4. **Update Website**
   - Current: instagram.com
   - **Change to: https://www.bosshookah.site**
   - Click "Save" or "Apply"

5. **Verify the Change**
   - Go to your Google Maps listing: https://maps.app.goo.gl/2sXDxnhukx8pzuW3A
   - Check that the website now shows www.bosshookah.site
   - Changes may take a few minutes to appear

3. Ensure you have "Owner" or "Manager" access to the profile
