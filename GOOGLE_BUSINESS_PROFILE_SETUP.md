# Google Business Profile Setup Guide
## The Hookah Shop

Setting up a Google Business Profile will help your business appear in:
- Google Search results for "hookah near me" and related queries
- Google Maps when people search for hookah shops
- Local pack (the map with 3 businesses shown at the top of search results)
- Google Shopping (if you add products)

---

## Step 1: Create Your Google Business Profile

1. **Go to Google Business Profile Manager**
   - Visit: https://business.google.com
   - Sign in with your Google account (use a business Gmail if possible)

2. **Add Your Business**
   - Click "Manage now" or "Add your business to Google"
   - Enter business name: **The Hookah Shop**
   - Click "Next"

3. **Choose Business Category**
   - Primary category: **Tobacco Shop** or **Hookah Lounge** (choose based on your business type)
   - You can add secondary categories later:
     - Vaporizer store
     - Wholesaler
     - Tobacco supplier

4. **Add Business Location**
   - **If you have a physical storefront customers visit:**
     - Select "Yes, I have a location customers can visit"
     - Enter your full business address
   
   - **If you're online-only or delivery/service area business:**
     - Select "No, I don't have a location customers can visit"
     - Enter your service area (cities/regions you serve)
     - Note: Online-only businesses have limited Google Business Profile features

5. **Add Contact Information**
   - Phone number: Your business phone
   - Website: https://www.bosshookah.site
   - Click "Next"

6. **Verification**
   - Google will ask to verify your business
   - **Verification methods:**
     - **Postcard** (most common): Google mails a postcard with verification code to your address (5-14 days)
     - **Phone** (if available): Receive code via call or text
     - **Email** (if available): Receive code via email
     - **Video verification** (sometimes offered): Record video of your business
   
   - Choose your verification method and complete the process

---

## Step 2: Complete Your Business Profile

Once verified (or while waiting for verification), complete your profile:

### Basic Information

1. **Business Hours**
   - Add your operating hours for each day
   - Mark special hours for holidays
   - If online-only, you can skip this

2. **Business Description** (750 characters max)
   ```
   The Hookah Shop is your premier destination for premium hookahs, shisha tobacco, vapes, charcoal, and smoking accessories. We offer an extensive selection of top brands including Al Fakher, Starbuzz, Geek Bar, and more. Whether you're a hookah enthusiast, lounge owner, or retailer, we provide quality products at competitive wholesale prices with fast shipping. Browse our collection of modern and traditional hookahs, exotic shisha flavors, natural coconut charcoal, disposable vapes, and essential accessories. Shop online at www.bosshookah.site for the best deals on hookah supplies.
   ```

3. **Opening Date**
   - Add when your business opened (helps with credibility)

### Attributes

Add relevant attributes to help customers:
- ✅ Online orders
- ✅ Delivery
- ✅ Curbside pickup (if applicable)
- ✅ In-store shopping (if applicable)
- ✅ Wheelchair accessible (if applicable)
- ✅ Credit cards accepted
- ✅ Debit cards accepted

### Products & Services

Add your main product categories:
- Premium Hookahs
- Shisha Tobacco
- Disposable Vapes
- Hookah Charcoal
- Hookah Accessories
- Wholesale Supplies

For each, add:
- Description
- Price range
- Photo

### Photos (VERY IMPORTANT for SEO)

Upload high-quality photos:
- **Logo**: Your Hookah Shop logo (already have favicon)
- **Cover photo**: Hero image of your products or store
- **Product photos**: 
  - Hookahs (Starbuzz Mini, various colors)
  - Shisha tobacco packages
  - Vapes
  - Charcoal products
  - Accessories
- **Interior photos** (if you have a physical store)
- **Team photos** (optional but builds trust)

**Minimum recommended**: 10-15 photos
**Optimal**: 20+ photos

### Contact Options

- **Website**: https://www.bosshookah.site
- **Phone**: Your business phone
- **Messaging**: Enable messaging so customers can text you directly from Google

### Additional Sections

1. **Q&A Section**
   - Proactively add common questions and answers:
     - "Do you offer wholesale pricing?" → "Yes, we offer competitive wholesale prices..."
     - "What brands do you carry?" → "We carry Al Fakher, Starbuzz, Geek Bar..."
     - "Do you ship nationwide?" → "Yes, we offer fast shipping across the US..."

2. **Posts** (like social media)
   - Create posts about:
     - New products
     - Sales and promotions
     - Product highlights
     - Tips and tricks
   - Post weekly for best results

## Step 3: Optimize for Local SEO

### Keywords to Include

Use these keywords naturally in your description, posts, and Q&A:
- Hookah shop
- Shisha tobacco
- Hookah wholesale
- Vape shop
- Hookah supplies
- [Your City] hookah
- Best hookah brands
- Hookah accessories
- Disposable vapes
- Hookah charcoal

### Get Reviews

**Reviews are THE most important ranking factor for local SEO!**

1. **Ask customers for reviews**
   - After purchase, send email: "Thanks for your order! Please leave us a review"
   - Include direct review link: https://g.page/r/[YOUR_PROFILE_ID]/review
   - Add review request on order confirmation page
   - Add review link in email signature

2. **Respond to ALL reviews**
   - Thank positive reviewers
   - Address negative reviews professionally
   - Include keywords naturally in responses

3. **Target**: Get 10-20 reviews in first 3 months

### Keep Profile Active

- Post weekly updates
- Add new photos monthly
- Update hours for holidays
- Respond to messages within 24 hours
- Answer Q&A questions promptly

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Don't:**
- Use PO Box as address (if claiming physical location)
- Stuff keywords unnaturally
- Create duplicate listings
- Use virtual office addresses
- Ignore reviews
- Let profile go stale

✅ **Do:**
- Keep information accurate and updated
- Add new photos regularly
- Respond to reviews promptly
- Post weekly updates
- Use real business address
- Be consistent across all platforms

- **Verification**: 5-14 days (postcard method)

## Verification Troubleshooting

**If verification postcard doesn't arrive:**
1. Wait full 14 days
2. Request new postcard
3. Try alternative verification method
4. Contact Google Business Profile support

**If you're online-only:**
- You can still create a profile with service area
- Limited features (no map pin)
- Focus on website link and phone number
